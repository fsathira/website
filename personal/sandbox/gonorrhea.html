Gonorrhea

a.k.a. the clap, the drip, and the dose

Symptoms

  • Frequently has no symptoms
  • Discharge from penis, vagina, or rectum and burning or itching during urination
  • Sore throat

Transmitted by

Gonorrhea is spread through contact with the penis, vagina, mouth, or anus. Ejaculation does not have to occur for gonorrhea to be transmitted or acquired.
