Biographical Sketch

Fah Sathirapongsasuti, PhD, is a Computational Biologist at 23andMe. He authored several scientific articles in the field of genomics and serves as an expert reviewer for high-impact journals such as Genome Research, Bioinformatics, and Nature. Prior to 23andMe, Fah received Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science form Stanford University and PhD in Biostatistics from Harvard University. When not in front of a computer, Fah can be found in the pool, on the lacrosse field, in the kitchen, or at wineries in Napa Valley.
